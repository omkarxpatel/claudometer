# Budgets

Set a monthly budget in **Claudometer Settings** (or `claudometer.budget.monthly`).

- The **This Month** card gains a progress bar that shifts from accent to amber (80%) to red (100%).
- The status bar adopts the same urgency colors.
- Alerts fire once at 80% and 100% of the budget.

Costs are **API-equivalent value** — subscription plans don't bill per token, so treat the budget as a usage gauge rather than an invoice.
