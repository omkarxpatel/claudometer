# The dashboard

Click the **⬡ cost item** in the status bar (bottom right) or run **Claudometer: Open Dashboard** from the Command Palette.

**Overview** — spend cards with change badges and month projection, live quota with burn forecasting, an activity chart (hour/day/week/month, tokens or cost), and a GitHub-style contribution calendar with your streaks.

**Projects** — every project ranked by cost. Click a row for a 30-day chart, per-model split, and stats. Projects open in the current window are tagged `open`.

**Models** — cost per model, the token/cost breakdown, and tool-use analytics.

**Sessions** — your most recent conversations with cost and tokens.
